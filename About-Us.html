Our Story

Riolo’s story starts like a cliched one with two graduate engineers who quit their jobs to solve a critical problem faced by our planet right now.  To understand the gravity of the situation you don’t have to go far but just to your nearest forest, river, or beach. For us the flash point was at our beaches in Mumbai where every week hundreds of volunteers get up on a Sunday morning to pick up the plastic trash coughed out by our oceans. So, in addition to cleaning up the beaches we thought of reducing the plastic out in the environment by recycling it into new appealing products. And thus, the idea of Riolo was formed. 

We are a Mumbai based company with our aim to create, support, and motivate a sustainable future for our society and to bring plastic waste into an organized sector. By buying a product from Riolo, people can do their bit to reduce the plastic waste entering a landfill or the ocean.

Tech

As a collaborative effort towards solving ​one of the problems faced by our planet, we have developed our technology with the help of the ​'Precious Plastic' project by One Army led by Mr. Dave Hakkens. 
All the technology and products developed to combat plastic pollution are open-source and available on the Precious Plastic Website.

We have further developed on the Precious Plastic Technologies to improve the efficiency, quality, and production capacity.
We aim to develop completely autonomous systems to maximize the amount of plastic recycled in our facility. 

Sustainability

Recycling by definition is a process of converting waste into reusable material. Recycling practices in India is quite common. Every household makes sure that their paper or metal waste reaches the recycling center through the raddiwalla model. The abundant availability of virgin or new plastic and low value of plastic waste has led to a situation where plastic recycling is neglected and the disposal of all that plastic became a problem.

We believe that sustainable development is something that companies should focus upon. Hence, at Riolo we aim to minimize our carbon footprint and make sure that the  benefit of recycling waste outweigh the ecological impact caused by the process. 

Single use product packaging is a major challenge in a world where plastic is the go to material for it. With our continuous research and develepment we aim towards solving the problem and hope in a future where our recycling rates for plastic are able to match to that of paper, aluminium, etc. and a successful circular economy model would be attained for our household waste.
